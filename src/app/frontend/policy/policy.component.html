About Eazyvenue

Eazyvenue is India's largest platform for discovering and booking banquet halls, event spaces, venues, and hotels for all kinds of events.

Overview

Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Mumbai, Eazyvenue is committed to simplifying the event venue search and booking process. We offer over 100 premium venues, hotels, and event spaces across India, catering to all kinds of events—from intimate gatherings to large corporate conferences.

Our platform allows clients to browse, compare, and book event spaces seamlessly. Every year, we expand our reach, serving over 200,000 satisfied clients and facilitating 500+ corporate events. Whether it's a wedding, a conference, or a special celebration, we have the right venue for you.
