Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common venue booking questions

We recommend booking your venue at least 6-12 months in advance for peak seasons (spring and fall) and 3-6 months for off-peak times. Popular venues tend to book up quickly, especially for weekends.

Basic venue rental typically includes tables, chairs, basic lighting, and access to restrooms. Additional services like catering, decorations, sound equipment, and extended hours may incur extra charges. Always check with the venue for specific inclusions.

Absolutely! We highly recommend scheduling a site visit before making your final decision. Most venues offer guided tours where you can see the space, ask questions, and envision your event. Contact us to arrange a viewing.

Cancellation policies vary by venue and are outlined in your booking agreement. Generally, there's a grace period for full refunds, followed by scaled refund amounts. We recommend purchasing event insurance for added protection.

Many venues offer in-house catering or have preferred vendor lists. Some venues require you to use their catering exclusively, while others allow outside caterers. We'll help you understand each venue's catering policies and options.

EazyVenue believes in transparent pricing. Common additional fees include service charges, gratuities, overtime fees, damage deposits, and vendor fees. We provide a detailed breakdown of all costs upfront so there are no surprises.
